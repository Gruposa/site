In the retail industry there is a problem called: "principal-agent dilemma", this occurs when a company depends on the actions and moral integrity of the seller. The dilemma exists because sometimes the seller is motivated to act in his own interest rather than those of the company.

While everything changes, innovation is a constant that gives a good sense of the quality and continuous improvement.
