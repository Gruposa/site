Minimum Value Product

Our goal is to overcome the "principal-agent problem" through a software that uses information gather from different sellers through the handheld device to provide valuable input to the company. We will use and exploit information recollected from the handheld device or mobile device making use of the geo –localization of the seller and with the objective of completing the processing of millions of records giving as a result of retail metrics through a digital dashboard that can esasily be accessed, used and interpret by the process manager.
